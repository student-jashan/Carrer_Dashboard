📈 GPA vs Starting Salary
Salary increases from GPA 2.8 → 3.1
Slight drop at GPA 3.2

👉 Interpretation:

There is a positive correlation between GPA and salary, but it’s not perfectly linear.
That dip at 3.2 tells you something important:
👉 GPA alone doesn’t guarantee higher salary — skills, field, internships likely play a role.

If you ignore this nuance, your analysis becomes shallow.
